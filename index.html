welcome to MAKEDUA

As-salamu Alaykum warahamatullah, These are the words of greetings which Islam has exhorted its followers to use at a time of meeting which have a deep spiritual and moral significance behind them. Since the predominant feature of Islam faith and practice is peace within and peace without, it has, therefore been a watchword which should be repeated on every occassion when two or more Muslims meet so that the optmost importance of peace should be constantly inculcated in the mind of every Muslim, as much, we welcome you to this amazing as you pray and greet your loved ones.

whyMAKEDUA?

When we do Dua, we submit that Allah is the only creator, He is Almighty and all is under His control. We are nothing before Him, we must ask Him.

"Whoever does Dua to Allah, He does research or in exchange avoids misfortune untill the dua is not related to sin or breaks any relationship"(Tirmidhi)

Dua has the power to change fate - Prophet(SAW) said "Dua diverts fate and good deeds lengthen the age."(Tirmidhi)

Dua opened the doors of Mercy - The Holy Prophet said: "For whoever has opened the door of Dua, for him the gates of mercy are opened."(Tirmidhi)
